About Me
My name is Nguyen Thuong Minh, and I am 16 years old. I am currently a student at Le Hong Phong High School for the Gifted in Ho Chi Minh City, where I specialize in advanced Math. From a young age, I have always been fascinated by numbers and problem-solving, which led me to pursue this field. In addition to my academic interests, I am also passionate about expanding my knowledge and skills beyond the classroom.
Looking toward the future, my dream is to study in the United States. I believe that studying in the US will provide me with unparalleled opportunities for both academic and personal growth. The diverse environment, cutting-edge research facilities, and emphasis on innovation will allow me to develop my potential to the fullest. I am excited about the possibility of gaining a world-class education and contributing to the global community in meaningful ways.
